About Ramen Naijiro

Ramen Naijiro: Authentic Japanese Street Ramen was started by Angelo Derek E. Limpo in 2018. It began as a small street ramen business, sharing the taste of real Japanese ramen with everyone. Through hard work and passion, Ramen Naijiro grew bigger and gained many loyal customers. Today, it is one of the most popular street ramen spots in the Philippines, loved for its delicious and authentic flavors.
